FAQ & Help

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the login page, fill in your details (name, email, phone, password, and role), and click submit. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

What information do I need to sign up?

You'll need your full name, email address, phone number, a secure password, and your role (Student, Teacher, or Staff). Make sure to use a valid campus email address.

Reporting Items

How do I report a lost or found item?

Log in to your account, click the "Report Item" button, select if it's Lost or Found, fill in the details (name, category, description, location), add a photo if available, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email with your item ID.

What details should I include when reporting an item?

Include the item name, category, detailed description (color, brand, condition, unique features), where you lost/found it, when it happened, and any contact information. Photos help significantly with matching!

Can I add photos to my report?

Yes! Photos are highly recommended as they significantly increase the chances of matching your item. You can add up to one photo during the initial report. Photos must be clear and show the item clearly.

Matching & Recovery

How does the matching system work?

Our system automatically matches Lost items with Found items based on keywords in descriptions, categories, and location. When a match is found, both users are notified via email and can contact each other to verify ownership and arrange pickup.

How will I be notified of a match?

You'll receive an email notification immediately when a match is found. You can also check your notifications in the app dashboard. The notification will include details about the matched item and the other user's contact information.

How do I claim my lost item?

When you find a match, contact the other user through the messaging system in the app. Verify ownership details, arrange a meeting at a safe location on campus, and complete the handoff. Mark the item as "Resolved" once recovered.

Account & Profile

How do I edit my profile?

Click on your profile icon at the top right, select "My Profile", and click "Edit Profile". Update your information and click "Save Changes". Your profile information helps other users identify and contact you.

How do I change my password?

Go to your profile, click on the settings/gear icon, and select "Change Password". Enter your current password and your new password twice for confirmation. A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support through the "Support & Feedback" section. We'll process your request within 30 days. Your data will be securely deleted, though item information may be retained for records.

Safety & Privacy

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take security seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We only share your contact information with matched items when you authorize it. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What should I do when meeting someone to exchange an item?

Always meet in a public location on campus, preferably during daytime hours. Ask the person to verify ownership by describing specific details of the item. If anything seems suspicious, contact campus security immediately.

Can I report a user?

Yes, if you encounter suspicious behavior or abuse, please use the "Report User" option or contact support immediately. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.
